Refactoring

Refactoring is a process developers use to re-write their code. There are lots of different reasons why you would want to refactor your code:

  • You want to make it easier to read
  • You want to make it more modular so you can use the different pieces in multiple projects and places
  • You want to make it run faster or have better performance
  • You know a lot more now than you did then, and you can make it better!
