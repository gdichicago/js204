Example: Fish

The class Fish has properties such as: color, size, canBeEatenByHumans.


And behaviors (methods): swim, eat, poop.


Child classes - Goldfish, Tuna, Clownfish - inherit from Fish.


All of these fish inherit the behaviors of swim, eat, and poop from the parent class Fish. They all have the properties color, size, canBeEatenByHumans.
